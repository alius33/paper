# Research Session Log

> Cumulative log of all research sessions conducted for The House of Paper.
> Each session records what was researched, what was found, what files were created or updated, and what gaps remain.
> This file is read at the start of every `/research` session to provide continuity across sessions.

---

## How to Read This Log

- Sessions are in **reverse chronological order** (newest first).
- Each entry records: the query, existing coverage found, new files created, key findings, and gaps.
- Use Ctrl+F or grep to search for topics across sessions.
- Cross-reference entries against `research-index.md` for file locations.
