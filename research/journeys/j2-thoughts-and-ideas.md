Talking about the road Yusuf takes to go to Kufa, it's mentioned that there's a "wider culture of competitive charity among the Abbasid elite"

So idea is this:

Either on this trip, or the next trip, he meets a minor noble, and through him he has access to people who are always looking to borrow money to show off to others

- The problem is that collecting from them is a nightmare, they have rich powerful parents and friends

- But they can be used for favours - if it came to be known that they were in deep debt, no matter who to, it would ruin their reputations

- Using this leverage, Yusuf is able to trade in their debt for favours - first it's introductions, then it's involvement in business deals only open to the rich and powerful (with Yusuf providing the money in secret)

- Eventually he climbs up enough to get into dealings with a barmakid
