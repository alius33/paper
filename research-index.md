# Research Index

> Master index of all research sources for The House of Paper.
> Updated whenever new research is added or new book digests are created.

## How to Use This Index

- **Writing a chapter:** Search this index for the chapter number, location, or topic. Follow the file paths to read the relevant research.
- **Adding research:** After creating a new research file or digest, add an entry to the appropriate section below.
- **Checking coverage:** Scan the "Research Gaps" section at the bottom for topics that need research before specific chapters can be written.
